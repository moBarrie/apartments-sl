# Revenue Strategy & Business Model

## 💰 Revenue Streams

### 1. Booking Commission (Primary)

**Model**: Take 10-15% commission on every booking

**Example Calculation**:

```
Apartment Price: $100/night × 3 nights = $300
Platform Fee (12%): $36
Landlord Receives: $264
```

**Projected Revenue**:

- 100 bookings/month × $36 avg commission = $3,600/month
- 500 bookings/month = $18,000/month
- 1000 bookings/month = $36,000/month

---

### 2. Featured Listings

**Model**: Landlords pay to promote their listings at top of search results

**Pricing Tiers**:

- Weekly Featured: $10
- Monthly Featured: $30
- Quarterly Featured: $75 (save $15)

**Benefits**:

- Appear in top 3 search results
- "Featured" badge
- Highlighted in map view
- 3-5x more visibility

**Projected Revenue**:

- 50 landlords × $30/month = $1,500/month

---

### 3. Service Fee (Renter-side)

**Model**: Add service fee to each booking for platform maintenance

**Pricing**:

- $5 for bookings under $100
- $10 for bookings $100-$300
- $15 for bookings over $300

**Why Renters Accept This**:

- Transparent pricing
- Covers customer support
- Trust & verification services
- Secure payments

---

### 4. Premium Landlord Subscription

**Model**: Monthly subscription for advanced tools

**Price**: $20/month or $200/year (save $40)

**Features**:

- Unlimited listings
- Advanced analytics dashboard
- Smart pricing suggestions
- Priority customer support
- Calendar sync (Google Calendar, Outlook)
- Early access to new features
- No commission on first 5 bookings/month

**Target**: Landlords with 3+ properties

**Projected Revenue**:

- 20 premium landlords × $20/month = $400/month

---

### 5. Enterprise Plan (Property Managers)

**Model**: For property management companies or landlords with 10+ properties

**Price**: $50/month or $500/year

**Features**:

- All Premium features
- Multi-user accounts (team management)
- API access
- Dedicated account manager
- Custom branding on listings
- Bulk upload tools
- Advanced reporting

**Target**: Property management companies

---

### 6. Local Business Advertising

**Model**: Promote relevant businesses to travelers

**Ad Placements**:

- Homepage banner: $200/month
- Search results sidebar: $100/month
- Booking confirmation page: $150/month
- Email newsletter: $300/campaign

**Ad Categories**:

- Restaurants
- Car rentals
- Tour guides
- Airport transfers
- Mobile money agents

**Projected Revenue**:

- 5 advertisers × $150 avg = $750/month

---

### 7. Verification Services

**Model**: Charge for enhanced verification

**Pricing**:

- Basic ID verification: Free
- Enhanced verification (+ address proof): $10
- Business license verification: $25

**Benefits for Landlords**:

- "Verified" badge
- Higher trust
- More bookings

---

### 8. Photography Service

**Model**: Professional photography for listings

**Pricing**:

- Basic package (10 photos): $50
- Premium package (20 photos + virtual tour): $100
- Drone photography (add-on): $30

**Why This Works**:

- Quality photos = 3x more bookings
- One-time service, recurring revenue
- Partner with local photographers

## 🎯 Strategies to Reach $1M Revenue

### Strategy 1: Focus on International Travelers

Sierra Leone tourism is growing. Target:

- Remote workers (1-3 month stays)
- NGO workers
- Business travelers
- Returning diaspora

**Why It Works**:

- Higher booking values
- Longer stays
- Less price-sensitive
- Need verified, safe accommodation

**Action Plan**:

1. Partner with airlines (Brussels Airlines, Royal Air Maroc)
2. SEO for "accommodation in Sierra Leone"
3. List on international travel forums

---

### Strategy 2: Partner with SendHome (Your Fintech Product!)

**Cross-Promotion Opportunity**:

**Scenario**:

- Customer uses SendHome to send money to Sierra Leone
- Offer: "Visiting Sierra Leone? Book verified apartments"
- Integration: Accept SendHome wallet for payments

**Benefits**:

- Shared user base
- Lower payment fees
- Trust (both brands)
- Remittance recipients can earn by listing apartments

**Potential Impact**:

- 10,000 SendHome users → 2% convert = 200 bookings/month
- Additional $7,000-10,000/month revenue

---

### Strategy 3: B2B Partnerships

**Target Companies**:

- Mining companies (need accommodation for expat workers)
- NGOs (short-term housing)
- Embassies
- International schools

**Offering**:

- Corporate accounts
- Invoice billing
- Dedicated support
- Vetted properties only

**Potential**:

- 5 corporate clients × $5,000/month = $25,000/month

---

### Strategy 4: Expand Services (Ecosystem)

Don't just be a rental platform. Add:

1. **Airport pickup service** (commission)
2. **Property management service** (full-service)
3. **Rental insurance** (partner with local insurer)
4. **Utility setup service** (help expats set up water, electricity)

**Each service = new revenue stream**
